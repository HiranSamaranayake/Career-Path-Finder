How to Find Entry level Jobs for School Leavers...?

You can find good jobs as an intern and gain valuable experience to add to your career. There are many ways to find internships and entry-level jobs as a school leaver, but you need to choose the right job that will help you in your future path and give you advantages in the industry. You might be an undergraduate student or someone developing your career and delaying further studies. Either way, you should have a job and gain experience..

  1. From web sites
    2. You can explore various websites to find entry-level jobs and internships by using specific keywords like "school leaver," "internships," and "entry-level" to refine your search. Since new opportunities are posted daily, make it a habit to check regularly and apply promptly to increase your chances of success.

    Here are some websites:

    • TOPJOBS.LK
    • IKMAN.LK
    • XPRESS.LK
    • JOBBER.LK
    • JOBPAL.LK
  2. Through linkedin
    3. Finding jobs through LinkedIn is highly effective,but you need to have enough connections and even if you have limited connections. You can actively search for professionals who are hiring, as they often share posts about open vacancies. This platform also offers access to unique and rare opportunities that might not be widely advertised elsewhere. Creating and maintaining a LinkedIn account is essential for leveraging these opportunities and building a professional network. check here - linkedin
  3. Through job factory agencies
    4. One way to explore job opportunities is by connecting with a job placement agency. These agencies usually charge a fee to take your biodata and notify you when they find a suitable vacancy. While this method involves some cost, it can be an effective and straightforward way to discover potential jobs. Additionally, it can provide valuable experience and insights into various career opportunities, helping you identify the path that best suits your goals.
  4. Personal Connections
    5. School leavers can find jobs through personal connections by networking with family, friends, and community members. Referrals from trusted sources often lead to hidden opportunities and build valuable relationships for career growth.

When you find a job that suitable for you, apply with a CV if you want to create a CV check here-How to make a CV
